S'Mores in a Bag

Description:

This S'Mores in a Bag recipe is more of a fun concept than a traditional recipe, but it's incredibly easy to make and perfect for your next cookout or camping trip. All the ingredients are portable, so you can easily bring them along. Simply roast some marshmallows, then toss them into a bag of snack cookies and chocolate for a delicious, mess-free twist on the classic treat. The result is a sweet, gooey dessert that everyone will love—no campfire required!

Ingredients:

  • 2 snack-sized bags of cookies, such as Nutter Butter®, Teddy Grahams®, Mini Oreos® or Nilla Wafers
  • 1 1/2 ounces chocolate candy bar, broken into pieces, such as Reese’s® Peanut Butter Cups or Hershey’s® chocolate bar
  • 6 jumbo marshmallows

Steps:

  1. Open snack bags and add chocolate candy pieces.
  2. Roast 3 marshmallows over a campfire or grill according to desired degree of doneness; add marshmallows to bag. Allow the warmth of the marshmallows to melt the chocolate, mix cookie-marshmallow mixture around slightly, and serve immediately.
